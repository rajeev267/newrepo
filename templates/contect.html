Government Polytechnic Dwarahat, located in the rural area of Almora district, is one of the leading polytechnics of Uttarakhand. Established in 1975-76 by the U.P. Government for rural development and employment, the institute is situated near the block office Dwarahat, approximately 75 km away from district headquarters Almora and 3 km from the main market of Dwarahat.

The institution is around 130 km away from Kathgodam railway station and 125 km from Ramnagar railway station. Public and private transportation facilities are available here. The distance between Dwarahat and Dehradun (state capital) is about 425 km. The Directorate of Technical Education is situated in Srinagar Garhwal.

The Board of Technical Education, Roorkee (Haridwar) conducts all institutional annual and semester exams.
